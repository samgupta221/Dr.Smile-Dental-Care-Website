FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have got answers. Here are some of the most common questions our patients ask.

Do you accept insurance for dental treatments?
Yes - we work with most major insurers for cashless claims on implants, surgery and orthodontics. Bring your policy details to your first visit and our front desk will verify coverage before treatment begins.
Is the first consultation really free?
Your first consultation, including a digital X-ray and a written treatment plan, is complimentary. You only pay once you decide to go ahead with treatment. No hidden charges, ever.
How painful is a root canal treatment?
With modern local anaesthesia and microscope-guided technique, most patients describe it as similar to getting a filling. We numb the area fully before starting and check in throughout the procedure.
Do you treat children? What is the right age?
Yes, from age 2 onward. Our pediatric room is designed to keep first visits short, friendly and low-pressure, with no judgment if a child needs a few extra minutes to settle in.
What are your clinic timings?
We are open Monday-Saturday, 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM, and Sunday 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM for emergencies only. Walk-ins are welcome but booking ahead avoids the wait.
